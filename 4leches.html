4 Leches

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 1 ⁄2 tsp. fine salt
  • 6 eggs at room temperature, separated
  • 1 1 ⁄4 cups sugar
  • 1/2- cup whole milk
  • 1 1 ⁄2 tbsp. dark rum
  • 2 tbsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 14- oz. can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 12- oz. can evaporate
  • 1- cup heavy cream
  • 14 - oz. jar dulce de leche
  • 2- teaspoon dark rum
  • 1/3- cup water

Preparation

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Spray a 9" × 13" baking pan with cake spray; set aside• Whisk flour, salt and baking powder in a medium bowl; set aside. Separate the egg whites from the egg yolk. In a large bowl whip the egg whites using a hand mixer until soft peaks form, this should take about 2 minutes. Then add sugar and continue beating the egg white mixture until a soft peak forms. Next add the egg yolk one at a time until finished. Alternate the milk and the flour mixture until all the ingredients have between fully incorporated Finally add vanilla, nutmeg and rum mix until full combined. Pour cake mixture into the prepared cake pan. Bake for about 30 minutes or until cake is lightly brown. Let it rest for about 20-30 minutes Whisk the three types of milk; evaporate milk, heavy cream and sweetened milk. Using a skewer poke holes in the cake extending all the way to the bottom of the cake. Pour the milk on the cake. Set aside in the fridge. Combine the caramel, water and rum- microwave for about 30 seconds-this makes it easy to mix. Pour or spread mixture on the cake cover with a plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve at least four hours or preferably over night
