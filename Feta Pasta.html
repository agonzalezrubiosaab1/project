Feta Pasta

Ingredients

  • Grape tomatoes
  • Block of Feta
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices and Herbs
  • Garclic
  • Pasta

Preparation:

Using a baking dish safe for the oven, add the tomatoes, olive oil, salt and pepper and mix to combine. Add the block of feta cheese to the center of the baking dish, and either flip it around a couple times to coat it with the olive oil and seasoning, or just add a touch more olive oil and a couple cracks of fresh black pepper. Bake it in the oven until the tomatoes burst and the feta looks melted (and your house smells heavenly!)As soon as it comes out of the oven, add some fresh minced garlic and fresh basil leaves. Mix it around immediately so that the garlic softens from the heat in the dish. Add cooked pasta on top and give everything a stir. The feta and tomatoes will essentially create a sauce for the pasta.
